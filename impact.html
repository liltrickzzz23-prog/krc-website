Positive Environmental Impact

Reuse is the greenest option there is.

Recycling a phone recovers some materials — but reusing one avoids the enormous energy and raw-material cost of manufacturing a replacement entirely. Every device we keep in service is a device that doesn't need to be made or buried.

  • Keeps toxic e-waste out of landfills and waterways
  • Avoids the carbon footprint of new manufacturing
  • Conserves rare-earth metals and finite resources

Lower Carbon Footprint

Extending a device's life is one of the most effective per-dollar carbon reductions available to a corporate sustainability program.
