Privacy Policy

Last updated . This template describes how Knowledge Reach Connections handles information submitted through this website.

Note for the organization: This is a general starter policy. Before publishing, have it reviewed by legal counsel and update it to reflect your actual data practices, form provider, and any applicable regulations (e.g., GDPR, CCPA).

1. Information We Collect

When you contact us or submit a partnership inquiry, we may collect information you voluntarily provide, including your name, organization, work email, phone number, role, and any details you include in your message.

2. How We Use Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Respond to partnership and donation inquiries
  • Coordinate device collection, logistics, and reporting
  • Send relevant updates about our programs when you have requested them
  • Improve our website and communications

3. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with trusted service providers who help us operate the website and our programs (for example, form-processing and email providers), only as needed to perform those services.

4. Form & Email Providers

Inquiries submitted through this website may be processed by a third-party form provider and delivered to our organizational email. Those providers process data under their own privacy terms.

5. Cookies & Analytics

This website may use basic analytics to understand how visitors use the site. You can control cookies through your browser settings. If analytics are added, this section should be updated to name the provider.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information submitted to us. No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Choices

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information you have provided by contacting us using the details below.

8. Children's Privacy

This website is intended for businesses and adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through this site.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "last updated" date above.
