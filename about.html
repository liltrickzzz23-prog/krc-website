Who We Are

Technology has a second life. So do the people it reaches.

Knowledge Reach Connections is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit on a mission to close two gaps at once — the digital divide and the mountain of electronic waste the world produces every year.

Our Mission

A working phone is a doorway.

Every year, millions of smartphones are removed from circulation as technology evolves and customers upgrade. While these phones may no longer meet domestic resale standards, the vast majority remain fully functional — capable of providing life-changing access to communication, education, and economic opportunity in underserved regions around the world.

We collect retired smartphones from major carriers and technology companies, securely refurbish or repurpose them, and distribute them through structured initiatives in developing countries — sold at a fraction of their original cost so communities with limited resources can finally reach the technology that has been out of reach.

What Drives Us

Built on three commitments.

Global Equity

Access to technology shouldn't depend on where you were born. We put working devices into the hands of people building a future with them.

Environmental Stewardship

Reuse beats recycling, and both beat landfill. Extending the life of a single device prevents waste and avoids the carbon cost of building a new one.

Radical Transparency

Donors and partners deserve to know exactly where devices and dollars go. We document the journey of every donation, end to end.

Where Support Goes
