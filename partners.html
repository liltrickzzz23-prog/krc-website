Why Partner

Four reasons it just makes sense.

Significant Tax Deductions

As a registered 501(c)(3), in-kind donations of retired devices to KRC may qualify for substantial charitable tax write-offs. Turn a depreciating asset and a disposal cost into a deduction. (Consult your tax advisor for treatment specific to your business.)

ESG & Sustainability Reporting

Diverting e-waste and extending device lifecycles produces measurable, documented environmental impact you can report in your sustainability and CSR disclosures.

Zero Disposal Liability

Skip the cost and compliance risk of certified e-waste disposal. We handle logistics, secure data destruction, and documentation end-to-end — with certificates for your records.

A Story Worth Telling

Give your customers, employees, and shareholders a tangible, human story of global impact tied directly to your brand and your products.
