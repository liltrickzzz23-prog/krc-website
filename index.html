The Opportunity

Two problems. One elegant solution.

The technology your company retires every upgrade cycle is exactly what under-resourced communities need most. We connect the two.

53M+

tonnes of e-waste a year

Electronics are the fastest-growing waste stream on the planet. Discarded phones leach toxins into soil and water, while manufacturing new devices burns through rare-earth metals and energy. Reuse is dramatically greener than recycling — and exponentially greener than landfill.

2.6B

people still offline

Across the developing world, billions remain cut off from the education, healthcare, banking, and economic tools the rest of us take for granted. A single working smartphone is often the only on-ramp to the modern digital economy a family will ever get.

How It Works

From your storeroom to someone's future.

A simple, secure, and fully documented pipeline built for corporate partners who need accountability at every step.

We Collect

We arrange pickup or prepaid shipping for your retired, surplus, or trade-in devices — at any volume, on your schedule.

We Secure

Every device is factory reset with a certified multi-pass data wipe — your old photos, accounts, and information are permanently erased.

We Refurbish

Functional phones are tested, cleaned, and prepared for reuse, creating local refurbishment and distribution jobs along the way.

We Distribute

Devices reach underserved communities at a fraction of retail cost. Proceeds fund education, food, and medical programs.

Donate With Confidence

Your data is gone before the phone leaves the building.

The number-one reason donations stall is fear of leftover data. We eliminate that fear entirely. Before any device is refurbished, it goes through a certified, documented wipe that meets industry data-sanitization standards.

  • Certified multi-pass factory reset on every single device
  • Permanent removal of photos, contacts, accounts & app data
  • Certificates of data destruction available for your records
  • Full chain-of-custody documentation for compliance teams

Certified Data Destruction

Donors receive documentation confirming every device was sanitized to standard before reuse — protecting your customers, your brand, and your peace of mind.

The Impact of Your Donations

One phone funds far more than connection.

Revenue from refurbished devices is reinvested directly into the communities we serve — supporting children and families through three core programs.

Education

  • Registration & school fees
  • Books and learning materials
  • Uniforms, clothing & shoes
  • Digital literacy & computer training

Food & Nutrition

  • Daily meals — breakfast, lunch & dinner
  • Food bank program
  • S.N.A.P. benefit support

Basic Medical Care

A three-level care program covering everything from over-the-counter medicine to broken bones, burns, dental, and infant & family services.

For Corporate Partners

Do well by doing good.

Partnering with us isn't charity that costs you — it's a strategic win for your balance sheet, your sustainability targets, and your brand.

01

Significant Tax Deductions

As a registered 501(c)(3), donations of retired devices to KRC may qualify for substantial charitable tax write-offs. Turn a depreciating asset and a disposal cost into a deduction.

02

Hit Your ESG & Sustainability Goals

Diverting e-waste and extending device lifecycles produces measurable, reportable environmental impact for your sustainability and CSR disclosures.

03

Eliminate Disposal Liability

Skip the cost and risk of certified e-waste disposal. We handle logistics, secure data destruction, and documentation end-to-end.

04

A Story Worth Telling

Give your customers, employees, and shareholders a tangible, human story of global impact tied directly to your brand.
